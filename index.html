A hyperenergetic ranting blend of dance-punk, 00's garage and modern post-punk

A hyperactive frontman and a danceable, explosive cocktail of indie punk and garage rock. Fans of Arctic Monkeys, The Hives, and Queens of the Stone Age will feel right at home.

Formed in 2024, Orangu Thangs won the Achterhoek band competition at the DRU in Ulft. This victory earned them a spot at the Huntenpop Festival. At the end of 2024, they released their debut single Kids Along The Shore, which hit the number 1 spot in the 3voor12 Gelderland Top 20 Releases of 2024. In Juli 2025, they launched their third single Small Peaks into the airwaves.
